Cottage Cheese Pancakes

Final Product with a dusting of powdered sugar

These cottage cheese pancakes are perfect to make on a slow morning.

Ingredients (for 3 servings):

  • Eggs (3)
  • Flour (1/4 cup)
  • Butter (2 tbs melted)
  • Salt (1/4 tsp)
  • Cottage Cheese (duh - 1 cup)

Directions:

  1. Strain the cottage cheese using a sieve or a similar tool until it dries out. Eta: 1 hr
  2. Using a mixing bowl, beat the eggs. Then combine with the cottage cheese, flour, melted butter, and salt.
  3. Cook the batter on medium-high heat until lightly browned.
