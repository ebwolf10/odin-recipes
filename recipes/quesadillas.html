Breakfast Quesadillas


A twist to your usual morning!

Ingredients:

  • Flour Tortillas
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Bacon/Sausage
  • Avocado (recommended)

Directions:

  1. Cook the meat, add the eggs to scramble, spice, and then add any extra desired veggies.
  2. Heat the tortillas, and then transfer the egg scramble, coating with cheese.
  3. Cover, flip, cut, and optionally serve with avocado.
